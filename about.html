About

POP is a social instant messaging app with a focus on speed and security.

It’s superfast, simple, secure and free. You can send an unlimited amount of messages, photos, videos and files of any type (.doc, .zip, .pdf, etc.).

POP groups have up to 10000 members and you can create channels to broadcast messages to an unlimited number of subscribers. We built POP to make messaging speedy and safe again, without the usual caveats.

In case you're more into pictures, POP has animated gif search, a state of the art photo editor and an open sticker platform (find some cool stickers here or here). What‘s more, there is no need to worry about disk space on your device. With POP's cloud support and cache management options, POP can take up nearly zero space on your phone.

POP can be used on smartphones, tablets, and PCs, and POP has supported iOS(6 and higher), Android(4 and higher). You can install it on a Windows or Mac OS. In other words, you can use POP everywhere with your own cell phone number on most devices.
